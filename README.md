# Condensate of the KonCEPT course material for amateur radio certificate

This repository keeps a condensate of the band plans, Q-codes, acronyms and
abbreviations for HAREC-level amateur radio certificate in Sweden.
The condensate is based on what is presented in the KonCEPT material by the
Swedish Society of Radio Amateurs (Swedish: <em>Sveriges Sändareamatörer</em>,
[https://ssa.se](https://ssa.se)), and is intended to assist newcomers to the
amateur radio hobby in their efforts studying for their certificate.

Although the condensate primarily has been compiled for the HAREC level, it is
the authors hope that it after the added modifications (in 2026) equally well
may assist studies at entry level (Swedish: <em>instegscertifikat</em>).
